Best Childcare Centre for your Kids

We have been babysitting and educating many children for many years and we have gained many experiences on how to handle them. We are assuring you that we are one of the best childcare centre in Penang. Nevertheless, our childcare centre is certified by Jabatan Kebajikan Malaysia (JKM). We serve to take good care of your children.

  • Excellent babysitting and teaching skills
  • Mini playground for the kids
  • Nutritious and healthy foods

Play Ground

Our centre has various types of toys and also our own mini playground for the children to play with.

Creativity

We teach the children how to be creative and expand their capabilities in any aspects.

Expert Educators and Babysitters

Our educators and babysitters are well-trained and have many experiences handling babies and children.

Healthy food

We serve nutritious and healthy foods for the growth of the children.

Educational Tour

Our services include educational tour for the benefits of the children.
